Review

Jaedan DeSimone october 22, 2025

The Amazing Spider-man 2 is a movie that I think was ahead of its time. I think it gets all of the action, humor, and emotion perfect and truly reflects a perfect comic accurate spiderman. I think this movies musical score is beautiful and truly makes you FEEL like spiderman himself. Emma Stone also portrays an amazing Gwen Stacy and her and Andrew Garfields chemistyry and dynamic on screen together is perfect.

My favorite actor in the movie and of all time is Andrew Garfield. I think he plays the best spiderman and peter parker. His humor and expression is just like spiderman and watching him takes me back to my childhood like im watching spiderman stright from the comics on the big screen. Also Andrew Garfield is a chad.
